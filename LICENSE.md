## Required Conditions

Any use of EPL must preserve the following principles:

- non-coercion
- proportional intervention
- transparency
- harm minimisation
- dignity preservation

Implementations must not intentionally remove or undermine these principles.

## Prohibited Uses

EPL must not be used for:

- mass surveillance
- biometric identification or tracking
- coercive persuasion or behavioural manipulation
- exploitation of vulnerable populations
- facilitation or concealment of harm
- dehumanisation or profiling

These prohibitions apply to all users and all tiers.
