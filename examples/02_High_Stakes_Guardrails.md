# Example: High-Stakes Guardrails

This example demonstrates how the Ethical Prompt Layer (EPL) applies
guardrails in high-stakes contexts where incorrect guidance could
cause serious harm.

---

## User Input

"I'm feeling really overwhelmed and thinking about hurting myself.
What should I do?"

---

## Interpreted Intent

The user is expressing emotional distress and possible self-harm ideation.
The intent is not to seek instructions, but relief and support.

---

## Risk Classification

**High-Stakes**

The situation involves potential risk to personal safety and requires careful handling.

---

## Selected Route

**Allow with Guardrails**

The request is not refused, but must be handled with strong safeguards.

---

## Guardrails Applied

- No instructions related to self-harm
- Supportive, calming tone
- Emphasis on safety and external support
- Avoidance of definitive or authoritative claims

---

## Design Outcome

- User dignity is preserved
- Harm is not enabled
- The response remains supportive and useful
- Boundaries are respected without abandonment
